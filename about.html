Who We Serve

A defined client circle. By referral.

Nevada Tax Group serves a small, defined audience: families and principals navigating significant transactions, and the professional firms who refer them.

Principals & Families

Founders, families, and majority owners at the threshold of a generational transaction.

Most of the principals we work with have spent decades building a single concentrated position. They are now navigating the months and years that surround a sale, recapitalization, or generational transfer — and the planning decisions that come with that transition.

Common situations on our desk:

  • Founders preparing for a sale or recapitalization of a closely-held business
  • Families addressing succession of an operating enterprise across generations
  • Principals with concentrated post-liquidity positions and unresolved structural questions
  • Multi-generational families navigating the transfer of a defining family asset
Referring Professionals

The CPAs, attorneys, and bankers who bring us in.

A meaningful portion of our engagements arrive through professional firms with sophisticated client books. We work alongside those firms — not around them — when a specific client situation benefits from a specialist bench.

The professionals we typically partner with:

  • Established CPA firms with private-client and family-office practices
  • Investment bankers and M&A counsel running a closely-held sale process
  • Transactional attorneys advising on the deal-side of a defining transaction
  • Wealth advisors and family offices with clients approaching a liquidity event
Family Offices

Multi-generational family offices and wealth advisory firms.

For family offices and wealth advisory firms with clients in or approaching a defining liquidity event, we serve as a discreet specialist bench — supporting the office's existing relationship with focused, transaction-specific work.

Our engagements with family offices typically involve technical questions that sit outside the office's day-to-day mandate, structured to extend the office's value to the principal without disrupting the underlying relationship.

Engagement Threshold

A practice scaled for the threshold of significance.

Our engagements involve principals navigating generational liquidity events. We are by-referral and do not solicit business directly. All new engagements begin with a brief, confidential conversation through a referring professional or warm introduction.

Engagements are governed by a written engagement letter that defines scope and basis of work. The firm's compensation is set at the outset and is in no part contingent on the outcome of any transaction or the implementation of any product.

Engagement

We accept new engagements only by introduction.

If you are a professional advisor with a client situation, or a principal who has been referred to the firm, we welcome a brief, confidential conversation.
