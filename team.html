Team

Strategic problem-solvers for complex situations.

Each member of the firm has spent the principal portion of their career working through the kinds of problems that arrive in the years before, during, and after a defining transaction.

Approach

Specialists, not generalists.

The firm is structured around a small number of specialists, each with deep experience in a defined area of pre-transaction strategy. We are not a law firm and we are not a wealth management firm. We are a bench of subject-matter advisors whose work is to think, in detail, about the planning that surrounds a single, generational transaction — and to do that work in coordination with the principal's existing CPAs, attorneys, and wealth advisors.

Each engagement is staffed personally. No matter is delegated to junior staff, and there is no separation between the people who scope a project and the people who do the work.
