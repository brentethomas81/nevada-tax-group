Posture

A focused practice for a defined audience.

We work by referral. Our engagements are technical in nature and built around a single principle: the planning that precedes a transaction is far more consequential than the planning that follows it.

The principals and families we work with have spent decades building a single concentrated position. The decisions they make in the months leading up to a sale, recapitalization, or succession will compound for a generation. Our role is to be the quiet, technical bench that thinks through those decisions alongside their existing CPAs, attorneys, and wealth advisors — extending those relationships, not replacing them.
