Most of the principals who find us are asking the same three questions.

  • "Is our current planning sophisticated enough for what's about to happen?"
  • "Are we leaving structural opportunities on the table?"
  • "Who do we trust to answer the questions our existing team can't reach?"

In our experience, they are usually right to ask.

Posture

Beyond your existing advisors. In coordination with them.

Most of our clients have already engaged sophisticated counsel and accountants. They are not looking for a new primary advisor.

They are looking for a specialist bench that thinks, in detail, about the planning that surrounds a single, generational transaction — and that does so quietly, alongside the team they already trust.

What We Do

Three areas of focus, organized around a single transaction.

All Services
01 · Pre-Transaction Strategy

The work that happens in the year before signing.

Strategic structural review of closely-held businesses, entity rationalization, and the questions that quietly compound in the months leading up to a transaction.

02 · Estate & Legacy

Generational planning around a concentrated position.

Trust structuring, family governance, and the slow, patient work of preparing the next generation for an asset they did not build.

03 · Exit Planning

Strategic counsel for principals at the threshold of a sale.

Pre-LOI posture review, sale-process readiness for the principal separate from the company, and post-closing structural rationalization.

The most expensive planning is the planning that arrives the week after the deal closes.
